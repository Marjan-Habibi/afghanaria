Our Academic Services

Afghan Aria School offers a variety of educational, sports, and extracurricular services that foster the overall growth of students.

📚 What We Offer

School Services

Computer Skills

Students are introduced to basic and advanced computer skills through hands-on learning.

Football

Our football program focuses on both individual skills and teamwork, promoting health and fitness.

Educational Gatherings

Students engage with various educational topics and have the chance to interact with peers and mentors.

Monthly Science Check-Up

Students participate in science-based assessments and hands-on experiments to enhance critical thinking.

Arts Programs

Our arts programs include painting, music, and theater to foster students' creativity and self-expression.

Foreign Language Classes

Students learn various foreign languages and expand their global communication skills.

Science Laboratory

Fully equipped modern laboratory for hands-on experiments in physics, chemistry, and biology.

Scientific Tours

Educational trips to museums, science centers, and cultural sites to enhance real-world learning.

Kids Clubs

Fun and educational clubs including robotics, drawing, storytelling, and science clubs for young learners.

Parent-Teacher Meetings

Regular meetings between parents and teachers to track student progress and discuss development strategies.

Kangaroo International Exam

Participation in the International Kangaroo Math Competition to challenge and inspire young mathematicians.

Morning Seminars

Daily morning assemblies with motivational talks, news sharing, and leadership skill development.
